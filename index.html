Who are we?

We are a robotics team that was founded in 2014 by students from Faculty of Engineering,
Alexandria University both from mechanical and electrical departments.

What is the main objective of the team?

The main objective of the team is to participate in the submarine competition (ROVs).

Why did we set up the team?

Participating in competitions is the best way to make us grow adn apply what we learn
The goal of the team is to create an environment conducive to to creativity and practicality.

And this is why our mission became the transfer of knowledge and experience from generation to generation,
which is prompted us to open a branch in the team for school students to participate with us in the competition.
